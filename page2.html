Welcome to our website about the San Antonio Zoo. This page is about the zoo and its history.

The San Antonio Zoo was founded in 1914 by George Washington Breckeridge. He was one of the leading citizens in San Antonio. Bison and other animals were the first in the park. The park was the first to offer cageless exhibits, allowing the people to see the animals in a way they hadn't before. The zoo got an aquarium in 1948 and the Nixon Bird House, named for Richard Nixon, was founded in 1966. The collection of birds at the house is one of the world's largest.

Today the zoo is one of the best in Texas, alongside the Dallas Zoo and the Houston Zoo. it is most famous for the Nixon Bird House, its Africa Live exhibit, and the attractions at the park. The coolest part of the park in my opinion is the underwater viewing area where you can see hippos and the other creatures. It also draws more than 1.1 million visitors in the park every year.
